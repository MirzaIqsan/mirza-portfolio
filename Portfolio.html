Welcome to my Work!

Design

Fundamentals of Design

This project involved creating concept sketches for a namecard design, where I experimented with image-warping techniques to creatively integrate words and the logo. I explored different typography treatments to reflect the brand's essence visually. After testing several color palettes, I ultimately chose a turquoise and black colorway, which provided a sleek, modern aesthetic while maintaining strong contrast for legibility and impact.

Development

Immersive Technologies

I created a virtual reality escape room in Unity 3D, set in a prison environment. Players have to solve puzzles in each room to unlock the next one, with four puzzles to get through in total. After completing all the puzzles, the final door opens, and players can escape the prison. This project challenged me to design an immersive, interactive experience, blending both creativity and technical skills.
